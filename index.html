Drawing 101

How to get started

1

You don’t need to get fancy with the materials. Start off with what fits into your budget and what you are comfortable with.

2

Have fun with your drawings! Be imaginative, use references, and also flip the art to see if it looks right.

3

Keep practicing! If you don’t start off with small habits, you won’t see improvement. Your skills may not be as nicely as what you compare to other’s but it will show your improvement. And that’s rewarding!
